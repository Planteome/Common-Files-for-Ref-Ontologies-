Syntax for database cross-references either within nodes in an ontology or within other PO documents, 
e.g. PO.defs, will be standardized and that, as far as possible, we use the  abbreviations used by 
the NCBI. See: http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/collab/db_xref.html. 
The syntax would then be: Database_Abbreviation:identifier (as recently proposed/adopted by GO).
